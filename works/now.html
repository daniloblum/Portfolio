NOW

An Information Architect study of NOW video-on-demand system on televisions.

About

NOW is a service only for NET TV and Claro TV subscribers. Its content includes movies and series. documentaries and more, which can be paid or free depending on your subscription plan with the TV operator. The TV can make its access through a decoder, a mobile app ( android and iOS), and a browser.

The evaluated system is NOW only for NET operator customers accessed by television.

Goal

Generate contributions that allow us to improve the usability of NOW, making users able to find your content more easily and with less effort.

Role

Final accessment for Usability and Information Architecture specialization course.

How does the content organization can affect their usability?

One video-on-demand system houses several types of content in large quantities, in addition to being frequently updated with entries and exists of content from its collection. This amount of information can overwhelm the user and consequently not find it easily.

The information must be organized in an easy way and according to what users have in mind.

Research

Online Questionnaire

It was the start of this research, so I could join some goals in one form:

  • person_search

    Find the users

  • psychology_alt

    Know their habits and behaviors with the system

  • 3p

    Recruit for the next steps

The form was created in 3 steps like a filter: for everyone that could answer, for NET TV operator customers, and NET NOW users. For those that weren’t NET TV operator customer but have another company subscription or even knows about video-on-demand systems, I also asked about their availability for the next steps.

Research

Cooperative Evaluation

I created some tasks for the users complete based in some hipoteses that I had. In this test I could:

  • visibility

    Observe how users use the system

  • edit_note

    Identify usability problems related to information architecture

  • move_down

    Propose improvements to identified problems

Research

Card Sorting

After to observe the users how they use the system, I needed to understand how they think about the organization contents and then propose a new taxonomy.

Some users were intited to reorganize the labels from the main categories and other two levels of hierarchy inside. Each label was a card and they were free to group and to name them, exclude and include cards.

Analisys and proposes

It is necessary that information is organized in an ease way and according to what usersd have in mind.

Assortment

List of "Action and Fiction" films within the "Films by Genre" category. It was propose some options to sort the content making the search more custom by the user.

Series category are presented in direct list on the main page. So I took advantage of the way that the system display the series and was break in two parts, “Most watched” and “Alphabetical order”.

Contents audio and subtitle

Until this research, the system had two options for the same content: with audio in portuguese or audio in english with portuguese subtitle. They were independent of each other. Although I had two different ways of thinking after aplying my two research methods I decided to merge the options in one content for the users.

You can read more about my analisys and my experience with the results of these methods here.

Kids category

The system presents some options right on the home screen as a kind of highlight, but the user can only select them after accessing the category. I cutted one step and created a simple grid navigated where the user can select and enter on these subcategories.
