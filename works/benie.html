Benie

Benie is a digital pharmacy focused on supplying medicines and personal care products directly to consumers, and helping them to manage their in-home pharmacies.

UI/UX Designer

About

Benie developed a simple and intuitive platform to rebuild the pharmacy experience. Unlike other conventional businesses, Benie fully integrated its operations and management through digital applications.

Their digital platform opened new opportunities in distribution, market development, private service, product mix, and pricing strategy. All this to create a novel and unique patient-focused experience.

Accomplishments

As a UX/UI designer I recreated their app’s interface, with new features. The app's new look incorporates elements of the company's rebranding.

The dynamics of the new interface significantly improved the user experience, and helped retain and attract new customers.

Design System

Benie underwent a thorough rebranding, with a new color pallete, shapes, and tone of voice. The new brand incorporated lighter colors, which were more suited to the company's mission. With the application already in use by their customers, it was necessary to adapt the existing components to the new style.

I worked with Adobe XD to organize the elements on mobile-style clipboards. The benefit from such design system is that I could check the elements out directly on my phone.

Feature

Price Magnifying Glass

The marketing department suggested a strategy to attract new customers and increase the average ticket. The goal was to engage customers to add more items to the cart, by providing them with a discount based on the lowest prices available on the market.

  • category

    The basket must have 3 or more different products to run the feature.

  • local_pharmacy

    The feature simulates the user's cart with prices from three of their main competitor pharmacies.

  • price_change

    If any of the competitors’ prices was lower, Benie would match the competitor’s price with a discount.

Feature

Digital Prescription

The recent increase in virtual medical appointments has led to an increase in digital prescriptions. Benie decided to exploit this opportunity implementing the following initiatives:

  • handshake

    Establish partnerships with companies that are developing their own platforms and APIs to support digital prescriptions, to insert a button “Order from Benie” to their platforms.

  • account_tree

    Use the information in a digital prescription to automatically add the prescribed medicines to the cart.

  • event_repeat

    Collect data about patients and medicines to automatically suggest recurring orders to their customers.

User flow

I created a user flow where the user can easily place an order with Benie straight from their digital prescription provider platform.
