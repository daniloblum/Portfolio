Benie

Tech company with digital service focused on the management and supply of home pharmacies.

About

Benie has developed a simple, more intuitive platform to rebuild the pharmacy experience.

Contrary to the conventional operation, its performance was 100% digital by its application. From it, the company saw opportunities in distribution, market development, private service, product mix, and pricing strategy. All this to create a fully patient-focused experience.

Goal

Redesign the app with the new branding and styles. Design new features to improve the user experience to attract new customers and make them recurrent.

Role

UX / UI Design

Design System

The company had a complete rebranding including new colors pallete, shapes and voice tone. The app was already built and avaiable for the costumers, so first of all I adapted the old component styles to a new one trying to be more simple and light.

Working with Adobe XD I had an idea to organize the elements in mobile artboards, so I could check them directly on my phone.

Feature

Price Magnifying Glass

One of the strategies to attract new costumers and grow up the average ticket was incentives the costumers to put more products on the basket (shopping cart).

  • visibility

    The basket must have 3 or more different products to run the feature.

  • edit_note

    The feature simulate the user's basket at other 3 main competitors pharmacies.

  • move_down

    If one of the found prices is lower, Benie cover the lowest value.

User flow

I made this user flow to visualize better how users interact with this feature.

Feature

Digital Prescription

The increase of virtual medical appointment and medicine’s online shopping, doctors are prescribing their patients with the digital prescription.

  • account_tree

    Bring all the patient information from the digital prescription and build a simple and easy flow to order their medicines.

  • event_repeat

    Patients information can be useful to know their needs and try to make them to create automatic recurring orders.

  • handshake

    Partnership with particular companies that are developing their own platforms and API. Insert a button “Order with Benie” in their platforms.

User flow

Create a simple flow where the user can make a specific search for each medicine in the list.

Layout

Responsive design

The users could access the app and buy from desktops devices, so using mobile first method, all interfaces was adapted to these devices too.
