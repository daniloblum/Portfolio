Dialog

A corporate platform for personal and professional internal integration. Engagement, communication, various services, and company management.

About

Many organizations do not have an internal integration between their employees. Dialog proposes to unify the services and information available to employees on a single platform, strengthening corporate communication.

I had the opportunity to collaborate on a short-term project to develop new system features.

Goal

Design new features for services available on the platform and organize the visual elements of the interface in a Design System.

Role

UX / UI Design

Design System

Due to being a product already launched and the short term for the execution of the project, the team was focused on delivering the demands requested by stakeholders. The work was collaborative and sometimes simultaneous in a single file. Therefore, there was a need to start organizing and documenting the interface elements for consistency.

Feature

Space reservation

Usually, facilities such as meeting rooms are the responsibility of one of the company's sectors. The feature allows the collaborator, with permission given by the administrator, to reserve these spaces on their own.

Some spaces were mapped and, according to their characteristics and needs, 3 different flows were created to carry out the reservation.
fastfoodRefectory

Companies offering on-site dining can organize the flow of employees and their particular times.

Useful information:

  • equipment;
  • permissions and prohibitions;
  • places available in real time;
  • period of meals offered.
computerWorkstation

Many companies have joined the hybrid work mode, where employees use collaborative stations.

Useful information:

  • what the location can offer and what it can restrict;

  • schedules by time of day.
